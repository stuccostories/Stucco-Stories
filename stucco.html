About

Buildings
that breathe

Stucco Stories is a visual exploration of the built environment — from the grandeur of monumental structures to the quiet intimacy of a well-worn interior. Based in Chennai, a city layered with colonial, Dravidian, and modern architecture.

The work documents architecture, interiors, materials, and texture — always through the lens of natural light and honest observation. No staging. No styling. Just spaces as they are.

Every crack in a wall, every patch of afternoon light on plaster, every shadow thrown by a column — these are the stories worth telling.
