About

Two worlds,
one eye

I'm Vaishali — an architect and interior designer based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. My work is rooted in material honesty, spatial light, and the quiet character of built things.

Stucco Stories is my architectural practice — renovation, interiors, heritage, and the careful documentation of spaces that hold memory.

From Ordinary Hours is what happens when I'm not at a drawing board — a casual photographer's eye turned toward the world around me. Architecture, people, places, light. Both practices share the same instinct: to notice what most people walk past.
